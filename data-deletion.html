Request Data Deletion

Simon Didn't Say — ThirdPaw Productions

You can request that we delete the data associated with your use of Simon Didn't Say, including your leaderboard display name and score.

How to Request Deletion

  1. Email us at thirdpawproductions@gmail.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request."
  2. Include the display name you use on the leaderboard so we can locate your record.
  3. We will delete your display name and score from the leaderboard and confirm by reply, typically within 7 days.

What Is Deleted

Upon request, we delete your leaderboard display name and associated score from our servers. Data collected by third-party services (Google AdMob and Google Analytics for Firebase) is managed by those providers; you can reset your device's Advertising ID at any time in Settings → Google → Ads to unlink prior ad data.

For more details, see our Privacy Policy.
