Privacy Policy

Simon Didn't Say — ThirdPaw Productions
Effective date: July 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how ThirdPaw Productions ("we," "us," or "our") handles information when you use the mobile game Simon Didn't Say (the "App"), available on Google Play.

Information We Collect

We do not ask for or collect any personal information such as your name, email address, or contact details. However, the App uses third-party services that may automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Player display name — a nickname you choose when you first play, which is stored online and shown on the in-game leaderboard alongside your score. We recommend not using your real name.
  • Device information — such as device model, operating system version, language, and screen size
  • Advertising identifiers — such as your device's Google Advertising ID
  • Usage data — such as gameplay sessions, session length, in-game events, and crash reports
  • Approximate location — inferred from IP address by advertising services for ad delivery (we do not access precise GPS location)

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, which collect information as described in their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob (advertising) — Google Privacy Policy · How Google uses data in AdMob
  • Google Analytics for Firebase (analytics) — Firebase Privacy and Security

How This Information Is Used

Information collected by these services is used to:

  • Display player-chosen nicknames and scores on the in-game leaderboard
  • Serve and measure advertisements within the App
  • Understand how players use the App so we can improve gameplay and fix bugs
  • Monitor App stability and performance

We do not sell your personal information.

Advertising Choices

You can opt out of personalized advertising on your device at any time. On Android, go to Settings → Google → Ads and enable "Delete advertising ID" or opt out of ad personalization.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided information through the App, please contact us and we will take steps to remove it.

Data Retention and Deletion

We do not store personal data on our own servers. Data collected by third-party services is retained according to their respective policies. To request deletion of data associated with your device, or removal of your display name and score from the leaderboard, you can reset your Advertising ID in your device settings or contact us using the details below.

Security

Information transmitted by the third-party services listed above is handled using industry-standard security practices. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, but we rely on reputable providers to safeguard the data they process.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
